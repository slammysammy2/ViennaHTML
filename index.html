Why Vienna?

Vienna, the city where the old meets the new

There has yet to be a city in Europe that relishes facades while simultaneously relishing even more about what’s behind the facade. Whether it be politics, a grumpy face, or a summer palace; there is always more than what meets the eye.

CAFES

My favorite cafes in Vienna

Burggasse 24

This cafe is both a vintage shop and a coffeehouse. You can peruse their collection of carefully selected pieces and/or find a cosy spot and cuddle up with a book. In the winter, they have a fireplace running and in the summer they have an outdoor seating.

Address:

Burggasse 24, 1070 Vienna

What I like about it

I feel like I could stay there all day and I have the feeling that I can be there for any reason. They have plenty of outlets to do some work, plenty of couches to read, and plenty of chairs and tables to chat with a friend.
